Introduction

Zhou Shen, born on September 29, 1992, in Shaoyang, Hunan, and raised in Guiyang, Guizhou, graduated from the Lviv National Musical Academy in Ukraine, majoring in vocal opera. He officially debuted in 2014 by participating in The Voice of China Season 3. His signature songs include Big Fish, China in the Lights, Little Happiness, What Is Human, and Pass By, among others. He is widely renowned for his clear and ethereal vocals, versatile voice, and a unique singing style that seamlessly blends classical opera with pop music.
