Monologue

Hi, I’m Ethan,

a music enthusiast from China,

a “Shengmi” who deeply loves Charlie Zhou Shen,

and a hardworking student.

Online, I might be known as

“Notes in Kabu’s Melody Bag”

(Chinese: 卡布米袋里的音符)

“Floating in the Night Sky”

(Chinese: 游夜空漂浮);

or more stylishly, “Kabu’s Melody Bag”;

or simply, “Zjewel8159”...

As a content creator,

I have over 3,000 followers on Bilibili.

“Vocal Pitch Analysis" is my primary content,

though I’ve been experimenting with new formats lately.

However, I keep a clear line between the internet and reality.

In real life, I am just Ethan,

an Ethan who tries his best to love this world—

it will get better.

Finally,

Love you, Charlie.

Always looking forward to our every encounter.

Love you all.
