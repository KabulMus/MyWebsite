About Me

Hi, I’m Ethan,

a music enthusiast from China,

a “Shengmi” who deeply loves Charlie Zhou Shen,

and a hardworking middle school student.

As a content creator,

I’m glad to have 3,000+ followers on Bilibili.

To me, creating videos is a way to unwind,

yet I always strive for excellence.

In reality, I am just Ethan,

the Ethan who loves this world with all his heart—

knowing that things will always get better.

Finally,

love you all, my friends.

Having each of you in my life is a blessing.

Love you, Charlie.

I will forever look forward to every encounter.
